## How It Works

### Step 1: Competitive Price Research & Data Collection
*Comprehensive competitor pricing landscape analysis*

Gather competitive pricing intelligence:
- Research competitor product catalogs and pricing structures across platforms
- Analyze price positioning relative to features, quality, and brand positioning
- Map pricing tiers and identify competitor clustering patterns
- Collect historical pricing data and promotional patterns

### Step 2: Market Analysis & Opportunity Identification
*Strategic pricing gap and positioning analysis*

Analyze market pricing dynamics:
- Identify underserved price points and market gaps
- Evaluate price-value relationships and competitive advantages
- Analyze customer price sensitivity and purchasing behavior patterns
- Assess market positioning opportunities and threats

### Step 3: Strategic Pricing Recommendations
*Data-driven pricing strategy development*

Develop pricing strategy recommendations:
- Create optimal pricing positioning based on competitive landscape
- Identify strategic pricing moves to capture market share
- Develop dynamic pricing strategies and promotional tactics
- Establish pricing monitoring and adjustment frameworks
